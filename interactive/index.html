CD Livewire

Diagnostic

Big Rocks is the transformation of CD in Developed Markets and aims at liberating CD from many of their repetitive/ administrative/ internally focussed activities so as to drive growth and efficiency. The aim is for CD in market to spend 80% of their time in externally focused activities.

This will be delivered by landing new processes centred around 4 core processes (Category, Promo, Execution, Plan), new tools and a new organisation, including the set-up of an ETS run CD shared services team. CD Livewire fits under the Plan process and aims to deliver role-based, process-aligned dashboards and analytics delivering one version of the truth and delivering efficiency for front-line CD to provide one-stop shop for anyone working in CD to access the latest information that they need to do their job whilst freeing up valuable time of our resources.

The solution is fully industrialized with dashboards refreshed daily, data flowing automatically from

internal systems, as well as ingesting external Nielsen and retailer data provided directly by our partners (This includes the main identified Core Data parts : ESRA, Finance Reporting, Nielsen data, Forecast & Targets and may incorporate other relevant data sources as required (TPM, ISCA..). It has been developed in Azure using PowerBI as the front-end solution, and the standardized dashboards quickly become pre-filtered to what each user needs, to allow them to zoom in, within minutes, to the areas that need their attention

  • Monitor performance in market with full visibility of sell-in, sell-out & financials
  • Free up time from back-office activity to focus on market facing activities
  • Full visibility on Retail Margins and Profit to support more focused investment and planning process + tracking and evaluation
  • Enhance and shape channel and shopper driven strategies
